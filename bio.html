Where I come from...

I was born in Bangalore, India and lived there till I was six years old. When I was six, I moved to Doha, Qatar and lived there for the short period of 6 months. I then moved back to India, but this time to Chennai - where both my parents are from. As you can see I was traveling a lot in my childhood, but at the age of 7 I moved to Singapore and I stayed there until I graduated from high school!

Professional Life

I am currently a senior at Tufts University majoring in General Psychology and minoring in Computer Science. I am interested in studying psychopathology, and I hope to pursue a career in clinical psychology following graduation. I am interested in the development of computer-based interventions and techniques for mental disorders. I currently intern at a cognitive neuroscience lab at Boston Children's Hospital on a project that is testing whether specially designed computer games can improve executive functioning in children with autism spectrum disorders. After I graduate, I plan on working in a psychology research lab for a year before applying to Clinical Psychology Ph.D programs.
